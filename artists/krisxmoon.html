The Artist

Chloe Kristensen

Fine Line Minimalist Whimsical Celestial

Chloe Kristensen, working under the name Krisxmoon, is a fine-line tattoo artist whose practice orbits the celestial, the whimsical, and the beautifully precise. Based at Tattoo Temple Houston, Chloe brings a jeweler's eye to her tattooing — every element placed with intention, every line a commitment to clarity and grace. Her work spans minimal single-needle pieces to intricate compositions featuring moons, stars, florals, and fantastical creatures rendered with a lightness of touch that belies the technical skill behind them.

Chloe's clients often describe her work as feeling magical — and not without reason. There is something in the combination of her subject matter and her technique that elevates even the smallest piece into something that feels charged, alive, and deeply personal. She is particularly skilled at designing for visibility: understanding how light falls, how skin moves, and how a tattoo will read ten years from now. If you are drawn to work that carries a sense of wonder — that finds the extraordinary in the delicate — Chloe is ready to collaborate. Reach out via Instagram DM to start the conversation.
