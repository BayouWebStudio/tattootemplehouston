About the Studio

A Temple Built on
Sacred Geometry

Tattoo Temple Houston was founded on a singular belief: that the human body is the most profound canvas in existence. Nestled in Houston, Texas, our studio brings together four artists whose work spans the full spectrum of precision tattooing — from large-scale sacred geometry and dot-work mandalas to ethereal fine-line illustration and dark, emotive imagery. Every piece that leaves this studio is a testament to craft, intention, and the transformative power of permanent art.

We specialize in styles that demand absolute technical mastery — geometric constructions that mirror the mathematical language of the universe, linework so fine it approaches the invisible, and compositions that command the eye across every plane of the body. Whether you arrive with a vision fully formed or only an instinct, our artists are collaborators at heart. We don't just tattoo — we translate.
