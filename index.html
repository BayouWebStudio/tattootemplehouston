The Studio

A Different Kind of Experience

Tattoo Temple Houston is a private, by-appointment studio specializing in geometric and sacred geometry tattoos. Every piece is custom designed and crafted for the individual.

No walk-ins — only dedicated sessions for clients who are serious about their art. When you book with us, you're not getting a flash design pulled from a binder. You're commissioning an original work.

The studio is quiet, focused, and built for long sessions. No distractions, no rushing — just you and your artist committed to creating something extraordinary.
