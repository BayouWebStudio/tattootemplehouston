About the Studio

Where Sacred
Geometry Becomes Art

Tattoo Temple Houston is the home of @weschetattoo — a private studio dedicated to the creation of large-scale geometric and sacred geometry tattoos of uncompromising quality.

For over 12 years, master artist Raul Wesche has been pushing the boundaries of geometric tattooing in Houston, TX. Each piece is a deeply personal, custom creation — an investment in art that lasts a lifetime.

We operate by appointment only. Our studio accepts a limited number of clients each week to ensure every project receives the full attention and artistry it deserves.
